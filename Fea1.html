Tips for Choosing a Water Jar Provider:

1. Water Quality: Ask about their water source, purification process, and any certifications they hold.

2. Delivery Rates and Schedule: Inquire about delivery fees, minimum order requirements, and delivery frequency options.

3. Jar Deposit or Rental: See if there's a deposit for the jars or if it's a rental system.

4. Customer Reviews: Read online reviews to get insights from other customers.
